Index Delighted Treehouse Monolith
Treehouse
Lead mobile product designer
Jun 2012 – Aug 2016

As the mobile designer at Treehouse, I was tasked with translating our core web app to iOS devices. In this process I worked alongside an iOS engineer. Together we established our own roadmap and produced an app which greatly simplified what existed on web.

The app retained a rating of at least 4.8 stars in the App Store during active development and was featured on various App Store highlights. Users commonly expressed that the iOS apps were preferable to the web app.

The courses taught how to code in various languages using video, code challenges, and quizzes.

We also built a standalone app for reviewing HTML. Since I was responsible for the content as well, we were able to better suit it to the device– producing custom keyboards to simplify the input of special characters.

Additionally, I handled illustration for the brand and produced various marketing pages.

I also illustrated hundreds of badges to represent users’ progress.
