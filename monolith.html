Monolith Foundry
Personal project
2020 – Now

I began designing typefaces in 2009 and would sell my amateur creations under the name Pilgrim Fonts. By 2020, I felt my designs had reached a different standard and I rebranded as Monolith Foundry. I built the shop app myself using Ruby on Rails and continue to maintain it.

The font view is just the essentials– an input field for testing the fonts and a payment form. This makes the app easier for me to maintain and update with new releases.

The account view offers a simple way to download the user’s purchases and see when the fonts were last updated.
